Delete Your Account

HFI Connect · Holy Fire Ministries

You can permanently delete your HFI Connect account and your associated data at any time, using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open HFI Connect and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us from your registered email address and we'll delete your account for you:

📧 holyfireministries.india@gmail.com

Please include the name/email used in the app. We verify the request and delete the account, typically within 7 days (and no later than 30 days).

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login
  • Your profile (name, photo, date of birth, anniversary, contact details)
  • Your prayer requests, comments, and reactions
  • Your saved notifications and push registration

Limited records may be retained only where required by law, and routine backups are purged on a rolling schedule. Bible bookmarks, highlights and notes are stored only on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

Questions?
